About Me

I'm from Redlands, CA originally, and I now live in Marina del Rey, CA. I worked for a year at a software company that makes HR software for the government, but I recently quit to pursue software development.

Someday I hope to own my own business, and I think learning to code could be helpful. If not, it's what I want to do for my career until I someday own my own business.

I want to travel the world, and I'm on well on my way. I've lived in Amsterdam in the Netherlands and Chiang Mai in Thailand for extended periods of time, and I have no intention of quitting my quest around the world. In fact, I hope that coding will allow me to live and work abroad. Maybe someday I'll actually be able to fulfill my dream of having Anthony Bourdain's job.


But that's enough about me! Feel free to read my posts to find out what I'm doing/thinking while I learn to become a developer at the Dev Boot Camp in NYC.
